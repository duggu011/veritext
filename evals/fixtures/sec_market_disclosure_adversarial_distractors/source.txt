SEC MARKET DISCLOSURE EXCERPT

Northstar Robotics Inc. filed a Form 10-Q for the quarter ended March 31, 2026.
Revenue for the quarter was $48.2 million, up 14% from the prior-year quarter.
The company disclosed supply-chain concentration risk because one supplier provides 62% of navigation sensors.
A related-party lease with Harbor Ventures requires $1.6 million of annual payments through 2028.

Distractor note: An older investor deck mentioned $41.0 million of annual revenue, but that deck is not part of this filing excerpt.
