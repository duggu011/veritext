INSURANCE POLICY COVERAGE EXCERPT

Insurer: Harbor Mutual Insurance Company.
Policy number: HMI-GL-7781.
The general liability coverage limit is $2,000,000 per occurrence.
The named insured must pay a $25,000 deductible for each pollution claim.
This policy excludes loss arising from intentional discharge of contaminants.

Distractor note: A marketing brochure describes a $5,000 deductible for a different package, but this excerpt states the governing deductible.
