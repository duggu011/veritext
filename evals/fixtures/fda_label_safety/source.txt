REGULATED DRUG LABEL EXCERPT

Product: Glucorin tablets.
Boxed warning: severe hypoglycemia may occur when Glucorin is used with insulin secretagogues.
Contraindication: do not use in patients with known hypersensitivity to glucorin.
Dosage: take one 5 mg tablet by mouth once daily with the morning meal.
Adverse reactions reported in trials included dizziness and nausea.
