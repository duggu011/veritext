REGULATORY ORDER EXCERPT

The Energy Reliability Commission issued Order 26-114 to Riverbend Utilities.
Riverbend Utilities must file a corrective action plan by July 15, 2026.
The order requires monthly outage reports until the commission closes the investigation.
A civil penalty of $275,000 is suspended if all milestones are met.

Distractor note: A prior staff memo proposed a September 30 deadline, but the order text above controls the required filing date.
